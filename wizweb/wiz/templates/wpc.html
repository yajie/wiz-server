WPC 2014 Conference review

The International Wood Plastic Composite conference was held in New Delhi on March 24th -25th 2014, which was organized by WIZ Business, and supported by WPC Union China and Suzhou JwellMachinery Co., Ltd. The leading players of WPC industries in Asia gathered at this event, discussing about the commercial and technical issues on this market. Altogether there were 17 speeches and one panel discussion from the leading players of WPC industry on this conference, which made this an informative and excellent conference.
